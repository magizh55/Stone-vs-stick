📖 How To Play

🎯 Objective

Align your 3 pieces in a straight line — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. First player to win 5 rounds wins the match!

🪙 Starting the Game

Tap Toss Coin to flip. The winner of the toss goes first. The winner of each round starts the next round.

🎮 Two Phases
PLACEMENT

Take turns placing your pieces on any empty node until all 6 pieces are on the board (3 each). If you complete a line during placement — you win that round instantly!

MOVEMENT

Once all pieces are placed, drag your piece to any adjacent connected node along the lines. You cannot jump over other pieces.

📜 Movement Rules
  • Move only one piece per turn
  • Pieces move along grid lines only
  • No diagonal movement on the board
  • Cannot jump over other pieces
  • Cannot move to an occupied node
🤖 AI Mode

Toggle AI: ON to play against the computer. AI plays as Stick and will try to win or block you.

🎨 Themes

Choose Forest, Mountain, Water or Fire to change the board's visual style.
